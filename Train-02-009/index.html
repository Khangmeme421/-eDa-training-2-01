Raw Material Testing

After preliminary testing of raw materials,
the next step is to bring samples for testing.
Compared to the importance of the stages,
this is the most important stage, committing
not to have any side effects and ensuring the
quality of the output products according to
the union’s standards.

These testing steps must be conducted
independently, meeting specific chemical,
physical and biological standards. Must be
checked until 100% of raw materials meet
the standards before being transferred to
the preliminary processing of herbs and
medicinal herbs.

All responsibility for published results rests
with the controller.
