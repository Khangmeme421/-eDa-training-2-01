WHAT IS BOOTHER DISEASE ?
• It is an inflammation and injury of the joints.
• It is a condition where the function of joints,
  ligaments, muscles, nerves, tendons and
  spine is impaired.
WHY OSTEOARTHRITIS ?
• The weather changes suddenly.
• Excessive exercise.
• Hard labor.
• Working in the wrong position,
SYMPTOMS OF OSTEOARTHRITIS ?
• Pain, swelling, stiffness.
• Reduced mobility.
PEOPLE WHO OFTEN GET SICK ?
The subjects most often suffer from the disease:
• Old person.
• Obese people.
• People with a genetic history.
• People with joint injuries.
